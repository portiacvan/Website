Visit Punaluu Black Sand Beach

Due to volcanic activity, Hawaiʻi's has both white and black sands. Found between Pāhala and Nāʻālehu in Kaʻū, it has striking black sands, coconut palms and you may even see Hawaiian Green Sea Turtles sunbathing. While swimming isn't recommended, there are picnic areas and restrooms.

Hawai'i's Tropical Botanical Garden

Frequently praised as one of Hawai'i's most beautiful places, this garden is a haven for photographers and nature enthusiasts. Boasting thousands of varieties of tropical flora sourced worldwide, it includes an orchid garden, a macaw aviary, and a scenic oceanfront path.
