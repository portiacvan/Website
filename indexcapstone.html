Explore Victoria

Visit Victoria

Welcome to Victoria, the charming capital city of British Columbia, nestled on the southern tip of Vancouver Island along Canada's Pacific coast. it's a vibrant hub of culture and history, boasting the title of "the Garden City". Victoria is a beloved destination for tourists.

History

Victoria, named after Queen Victoria, is known for its well-preserved historic buildings, including the iconic Parliament Buildings and the elegant Empress Hotel.

Before European sailors arrived in the late 1700s, the Greater Victoria region was inhabited by various Coast Salish communities, including the Lək̓ʷəŋən (Lekwungen) and W̱SÁNEĆ (Saanich) peoples.

Exploration of the area by Spanish and British navigators began in the late 1700s, with Juan Pérez's visits in 1774 and James Cook's in 1778.

Although the Victoria area wasn't explored until 1790, Spanish sailors frequented Esquimalt Harbour, just west of Victoria.

Famous Destinations

Butchart Gardens

Butchart Gardens is a botanical wonderland spanning 55 acres. With vibrant blooms, cascading waterfalls, and tranquil pathways, it offers a serene escape into nature's embrace. From the fragrant Rose Garden to the peaceful Japanese Garden, it captivates visitors year-round. A must-see destination in Victoria, Butchart Gardens promises a rejuvenating experience amidst its breathtaking floral displays and scenic beauty.

Dallas Road

Stretching along the coastline of Victoria, BC, Canada, Dallas Road offers captivating views of the Pacific Ocean and rugged shoreline. It's an ideal spot for a leisurely stroll, bike ride, or simply to soak in the natural beauty. With its picturesque vistas, charming beaches, and opportunities for wildlife sightings, Dallas Road beckons travelers to experience the breathtaking coastal allure of Victoria.

Downtown Inner Harbour

Victoria's Downtown Inner Harbour is a vibrant hub of culture and entertainment, boasting picturesque views of the iconic Parliament Buildings and bustling waterfront. Amidst the lively atmosphere, talented buskers add to the charm with their performances. Visitors can enjoy a leisurely stroll, take in the stunning architecture, and immerse themselves in the lively street performances, making it a must-see destination for a memorable experience in Victoria
